Friday Five: 417's Best Events 2/28-3/5

Share

I Smell Spring

As we are approaching March, it's time for some spring cleaning, a change in wardrobe, and new decorations. Reward yourselves for the hard work at home and go have some fun! We have all you need for a successful weekend! Check it out in this week's blog post!

What's Happening This Week?

Bull Blast 2020

Where: Ozark Empire Fair, 3001 N Grant Ave, Springfield, MO

When: Saturday, February 28 @ 8 PM - February 29th @ 8 PM

"29th annual Bull Blast. Family-friendly bull riding entertainment that will keep you on the edge of your seat! Fri Feb 28th and Sat Feb 29th 8:00 pm nightly."

Click here to get your tickets!

Winter Jam 2020 - Springfield, MO

Where: JQH Arena

When: Sunday, March 1st from 6 PM - 10 PM (Doors open at 5)

Winter Jam is coming to Springfield, MO on Sunday, March 1st! We will be at the JQH Arena! Join Crowder, Hillsong Young & Free, Andy Mineo, and many more awesome artists, with special guest speaker Greg Stier! + Pre Jam Party with Riley Clemmons, Ballenger & Zauntee!

•
Jam Nation Doors: 3:30PM
GA Doors: 5PM
Show Time: 6PM

Click here to get your tickets!

BOGO Flash Sale

Where: Word on the Street Books, 127 East Church Street, Ozark, MO

When: Saturday, February 29th from 10 AM - 5 PM

Did you know there is a new book store on Ozark's Square?! Take the day to come to check out all they have to offer...and Enjoy a sale!

"ONE DAY ONLY! Buy a book, new or used, and get a used book of equal or lesser value FREE"

Create & Take - Whiskey Tango

Where: Whiskey Tango, 1111 E. Republic Road, Suite 180, Springfield, MO

When: Thursday, March 5th from 6-8 PM

Cost: $30

"Join us for a night of fun March 5th, from 6-8pm at Whiskey Tango, to create your very own drink caddy/centerpiece! Enjoy some of the most delicious drinks in town while creating your very own project! This is one of our most versatile projects to date! With a chalkboard top, these are perfect for trying new flights, or even a small deck garden! We can't wait to see what unique spin you will create! If this is your first time building, don't worry! We will have staff on-site to help you along the way. We will be staining/painting our caddies, assembling, (we do use power tools for this portion!) & attaching black hardware!

** Seats are limited and you must be 21+ to attend this class

Click here to get your tickets!

Mother and Son Fun Night

Where: Ozark Community Center

When: Friday, February 28th from 6-9 PM

Join us for a night of excitement - dodge-ball and kickball, rock-wall climbing, pizza, and more! The event takes place at The OC from 6PM to 9PM. For each mother-son duo, the cost of the program is $20 dollars for Annual Family Members, and $25 for non-members. Each additional person is $5. Aunts and Grandmas are more than welcome as well! We ask that only individuals ages 5 and up attend.
